FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
ChatGPT answers questions. NSAI runs your operations. It's always on, connected to your real tools (email, calendar, browser, APIs), remembers everything, and takes action — not just gives advice. Think of it as the difference between a search engine and an employee.
Is my data secure?
NSAI runs on your device or dedicated infrastructure — your data never touches shared servers. All communication is encrypted. You have full control over what NSAI can access and what it can't.
What devices does it run on?
Any Linux-capable device: a spare phone (Android), a Raspberry Pi, a laptop, a VPS, or our hosted infrastructure. We help you choose the best option for your needs.
How long does setup take?
Most clients are fully operational within 48 hours of the initial consultation. The first week includes hands-on onboarding and optimization.
Can I try it before committing?
Yes — we offer a free consultation where we'll demo NSAI live with your actual use case, so you can see exactly what it would do for you before paying anything.
What happens if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime, no questions asked. Your data and workflows are yours — we'll help you export everything.
