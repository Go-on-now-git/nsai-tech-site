# NSAI Live Demo Script
### For prospect calls and screen recordings

---

## Setup Before Demo
- Have Telegram open on phone showing NSAI conversation
- Have the showcase site open in browser
- Prepare a prospect-relevant scenario (their industry)

---

## Demo Flow (15 minutes)

### 1. The Hook (2 min)
> "Let me show you something. I'm going to send a message to my phone right now."

*Send a message to NSAI on Telegram:*
> "Give me a quick summary of [prospect's company] — what they do, recent news, and their main competitors."

*NSAI responds in real-time with a researched answer.*

> "That just happened live. No pre-scripted. That's my AI operator — it has access to web search, browser automation, and it knows my business context."

### 2. The Daily Operator (3 min)
> "Here's what my morning looks like."

*Show Telegram conversation from this morning — NSAI proactively:*
- Checked inbox, summarized important emails
- Flagged a calendar conflict
- Pulled market data for a meeting

> "I didn't ask for any of that. It runs on a heartbeat — checks in every 30 minutes, handles what it can, alerts me on what it can't."

### 3. Automation in Action (3 min)
> "Watch this."

*Send NSAI a task:*
> "Research the top 5 [industry-relevant thing] and put together a comparison for me."

*While it works, explain:*
- Runs on a phone (or any device)
- Data stays on your hardware
- Connected to Telegram, WhatsApp, Discord, Slack, Signal
- Remembers everything — builds context over time

*Show the response coming in — real research, formatted, actionable.*

### 4. The "How Is This Different" (3 min)
> "You might be thinking — I can do that with ChatGPT."

*Pull up ChatGPT side by side:*
- "ChatGPT doesn't know your calendar. NSAI does."
- "ChatGPT forgets everything. NSAI remembers your last 6 months."
- "ChatGPT answers. NSAI acts — it can send emails, trigger workflows, automate your browser."
- "ChatGPT is a tool you visit. NSAI is an operator that works for you."

### 5. Their Use Case (3 min)
> "So let me ask you — what's the thing you spend the most time on that you wish you didn't?"

*Listen. Then describe exactly how NSAI would handle it.*

Be specific. Use their words back to them.

### 6. Close (1 min)
> "We build this custom for each client. 48 hours from consultation to running. $297 to start, $597 for the full operator package. Cancel anytime."
> "Want us to build yours?"

---

## Objection Handling

**"Isn't this just ChatGPT with extra steps?"**
> "ChatGPT is a search bar with better grammar. NSAI is an employee that never sleeps. It's connected to your real tools, runs on your device, and takes action — not just gives advice."

**"Is it secure?"**
> "Runs on your hardware. Your data never hits shared servers. You control what it accesses. It's more secure than any cloud AI tool you're using now."

**"What if it makes a mistake?"**
> "It's configured with safety boundaries. External actions (emails, posts) require confirmation. Internal work (research, organizing, monitoring) runs freely. You set the guardrails."

**"That's expensive."**
> "A part-time VA is $2-3K/month, needs training, takes vacation, and still makes mistakes. This is $297-597/month, works 24/7, and gets smarter every week."

## Recording Tips (for video demos)
- Screen record the terminal + Telegram side by side
- Keep messages short and punchy — viewer attention drops after 60 seconds
- Add captions — many people watch without sound
- End with the website URL and CTA
- Aim for 2-3 minute cut for social media, full 15 min for website
